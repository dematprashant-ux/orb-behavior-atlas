# 1. Mission

Build a research-first platform that discovers, validates, and monitors statistically significant market behaviors around the BANKNIFTY Opening Range Breakout (ORB), transforming validated evidence into production-ready trading strategies.

---

# 2. Vision

Develop a self-improving quantitative research laboratory where every trading strategy is derived from validated market behavior rather than intuition.
